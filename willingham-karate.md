# Karate Classes Near Willingham

Looking for karate classes near Willingham? **Northstowe Karate Club** offers traditional Shotokan Karate for ages 8 and above. We’re a family-friendly dojo focused on discipline, confidence, and fitness.

## Why Choose Northstowe Karate Club?
- Experienced Chief Instructor (4th Dan JKA)
- Inclusive and welcoming environment
- Free trial class for beginners
- Convenient location near Willingham

## How to Get Here from Willingham
Northstowe Karate Club is just **10 minutes from Willingham via B1050 and Station Road**. There's some parking on site, with additional parking available at Pathfinder Primary School, a short walk away.

# Upcoming Events

Our club will attend various events both at the club and international level. Key events are listed below - for a full list see [JSKE Events Page](https://www.jske.co.uk/events).

## 2026

### 5th JSKE Spring Course - *Saturday 14th March*

This is a fantastic opportunity for **all grades** to train with senior instructors including Kawasoe sensei 8th Dan, Tomlin sensei 7th Dan and Hori sensei 6th Dan.

### Kyu Grading - *21 March*

There will be a joint grading at Cambridge Karate Dojo on Saturday 21st March, where (if ready) you will be able to test for your next belt.

### JKA WF England Spring Course - *8-10th May* 

JKA WF England Spring Gasshuku at Windsor Leisure Centre with Kawasoe Sensei, Izumiya Sensei and Nemoto Sensei. This is another excellent opportunity to train with Kawasoe sensei, as well as instructors from JKA HQ. It's primarily aimed at brown belt and above but lower grades can also attend.

### Kyu Grading - *20th June*

There will be a joint grading at Cambridge Karate Dojo on Saturday 20th June, where (if ready) you will be able to test for your next belt.

### JKA WF England International Summer Camp - *7-9th August*

The annual summer camp in Paisley, Scotland is another chance to train with senior instructors from JKA HQ.

### Kyu Grading - *5th Dec*

There will be a joint grading at Cambridge Karate Dojo on Saturday 5th December, where (if ready) you will be able to test for your next belt.

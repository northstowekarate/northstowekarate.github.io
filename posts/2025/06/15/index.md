# Lesson Summary - 15th June 2025

We started off with some basics paying particular interest to oi zuki, stepping punch; age uke, rising block, and soto uke outside block. These are used in sanbon and gohon kumite - 3 and 5 step sparring. We then paired up with each other and practiced blocking each others attacks.

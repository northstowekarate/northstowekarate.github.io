# Grading — Cambridge — 21 Mar 2026

## **Overview:**
A joint kyu grading for eligible students from our club and partner dojos. This is the formal assessment for belts and will be run by Cambridge Karate Dojo officials.

This was a fantastic event. We had two sessions with Roy Tomlin sensei (advanced and beginner), followed by a grading. A total of 95 students graded (25 from Northstowe Karate Club) - all students passed. Well done.

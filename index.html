Northstowe Karate Club

Northstowe Karate Club opened Wednesday 3rd July 2024. We teach a traditional Japanese martial art - Shotokan Karate. Sessions will be held every Wednesday 1830-1930 at The Cabin. It's a family friendly club, where children (6+) and adults can both train together.

Your first lesson is always free! To book your first class please get in touch or turn up to one of our sessions. Please arrive 5 minutes early for your first session so you can be registered.

For more information and updates check us out on Facebook and Instagram

Northstowe Karate Club

About

Northstowe Karate Club will open Wednesday 3rd July 2024, running a 10 week traditional Shotokan Karate course. Sessions will be held every Wednesday 1830-1930 at The Cabin. It's a family friendly club, where children (6+) and adults can both train together. You come to as many sessions as you like, but we recommend coming to as many as you can, so you get a good idea of what Shotokan Karate is all about.

Your first lesson is always free! To register interest please get in touch.

For more information and updates check us out on Facebook and Instagram

Northstowe Karate Club

About

Northstowe Karate Club will open in summer 2024, running a 10 week course teaching traditional shotokan karate. The instructor is a 4th dan black belt, affiliated with the Japan Karate Association, one the oldest and most prestigious karate organisations in the world.

Trial Course

The club will be a running a 10 week trial course in Northstowe, summer 2024, dates to be confirmed. Your first lesson is always free! To register interest please get in touch.
